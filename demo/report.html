Commentary

Revenue growth was driven primarily by enterprise expansion, which contributed roughly two thirds of the new ARR added this quarter. The mid-market segment was flat, which we attribute to the seasonal pause in procurement cycles.

On the customer-success side, gross retention held steady at 94%, while net retention climbed to 117% on the back of strong upsell activity.

Outlook

For Q2 we expect continued momentum in enterprise, with three large deals already in late-stage negotiation. We are also rolling out the redesigned onboarding flow, which early data suggests will lift activation by 8 to 12 percent.
